Secret Links

This is a secret page on my website especially for you. Here I can share videos & recordings from the most exciting projects I've worked on, some of which are not authorized for wide public dissemination. I'll update this page periodically (& let you know when anything particularly thrilling gets added). Click on the underlined text to access the various media.


Skylark — Sub Rosa

Skylark is a fast-rising professional choir (with singers from all over the United States) that has been nominated for 3 Grammy awards in the last 4 years. This is a choral concert of music embedded with hidden meanings & secret codes! Composer Gregory Brown—brother of superstar novelist & occult-enthusiast Dan Brown (of The Davinci Code)—is emcee.


Ensemble Altera — We Remember

Ensemble Altéra (with that accent added by me to clarify the pronunciation) is a recently-formed professional choir based in Providence, Rhode Island, directed by internationally-acclaimed operatic countertenor Christopher Lowrey. This is a choral program of music about remembering and honoring the dead, conceived as a tribute to lives lost due to COVID-19.


The Advent Choir — Nelson Mass & more

The Advent Choir is the choir of the Church of the Advent in Boston. On June 5th, 2022 (to celebrate the church feast of Pentecost) we performed Haydn's Nelson Mass with orchestra, along with some other choral repertoire. Check out Schuetky's Emitte spiritum tuum, a great little-known piece.


Emmanuel Music — BWV 1 & Morning Star

Emmanuel Music is the "ensemble in residence" at Emmanuel Church in Boston, & is famous for presenting a different Bach cantata every Sunday. This is a world premiere performance of an a cappella choral work called Morning Star by Kati Agócs, immediately followed by Bach's beautifully lyrical cantata BWV 1.
